• Please find attached steps for to pay Institute Fee and Hostel Fee through SBI Collect.

• Read the document and instructions carefully before you pay the fees.



1.Fee payment window will be open from Jun 21, 2020 to July 22, 2020. Late fee will be charged amounting to Rs.1,000 from July 23, 2020 to 26th July 2020.

2.Fee must be paid by SBI Collect (Only those students who have taken education loan, can pay using DD). You must bring payment acknowledgement at institute when you report for registration.

3.Any charges levied by banks for fee payment, will be paid by students.

4.We encourage you pay USING INTERNET BANKING MODE AS MUCH CHEAPER AS COMPARE TO OTHER MODE.

5.All the details will be filled in CAPITAL LETTERS

6.If you are using a different flow or link to reach portal, do not worry, make sure you see IIITK logo and steps before fee payment.

7.Do not use Rupay Debit card, there is a known issue with this for payment settlement.

8.If transaction fails for some reason and amount is not deducted, wait for some time and retry.

9.If transaction fails, amount is deducted and you do not get reference number, contact SBI Bank.

10.If there is any issue with your payment details or for general queries related to fee payment contact IIITK office at office@iiitkota.ac.in / accounts@iiitkota.ac.in or 0141-2715070/141-2715071.

11.For technical queries related to the portal, contact SBI MNIT Campus branch at 0141-2529111.


• Education Loan: Only those students who have taken education loan, can pay using DD drawn in favor of Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kota (Payable at Jaipur)
• Link to SB Collect online portal for fee payment: https://www.onlinesbi.com/sbicollect/icollecthome.htm
